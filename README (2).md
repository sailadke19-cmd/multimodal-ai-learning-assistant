## Abstract
The Multimodal AI Learning Assistant is an AI powered platform to help students learn from text, pdfs, videos and voice records. It uses Whisper for speech-to-text, OCR to extract text from scanned documents, and LLMs (Large Language Models) with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to understand uploaded content. Notes, summaries, important questions, revision quizzes and answers are auto-generated by the system from the learning material. It is combining a number of AI technologies into one platform to reduce the time required for study, to increase understanding and to facilitate personalized learning. This project shows the possibilities of AI to make education more efficient, interactive and accessible, by providing intelligent assistance for students while they’re learning.

## Introduction
The students of today receive study materials in the form of lecture videos, notes in PDF format, textbooks, audio recordings, and presentations. It takes a lot of time and effort to manage all of these resources and make sense of them. “Most of today’s learning platforms only support one kind of content, making it difficult for students to organize and review their study materials.

The Multimodal AI Learning Assistant solves this problem by providing a single platform capable of understanding multiple input formats. “Students only need to upload their lecture materials and the AI will automatically extract the content, understand it and generate notes, summaries, important questions, quizzes and answers. This helps students to revise quickly and enhances their overall learning experience.
## Literature review
Many educational systems that work with AI can generate notes or answer questions about text documents. Speech recognition models such as Whisper can be used to accurately convert lecture audio to text, and OCR technologies can extract information from scanned documents and images. Great progress has been made in summarization and content generation with large language models (LLMs). Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) improves the reliability of AI responses by retrieving relevant information from uploaded documents before generating answers. But very few systems combine all these technologies in one platform and can process text, PDF, video and voice recordings together. This project is a complete multi-modal learning solution and fills that gap.

## Methodology
The user logs into the platform and uploads learning materials such as PDFs, lecture videos, voice recordings or text files. Whisper is text to audio, OCR is audio to text/PDF. Extracted content is cleaned, chunked, and transformed into embeddings. These embeddings are saved in a vector database (FAISS). As the user interacts with the system, the RAG extracts the most relevant information from the stored content, and the Large Language Model creates notes, summaries, important questions, quizzes, and accurate answers based on the uploaded materials.

## Results and Discussion
The system generates excellent instructional content by processing various kinds of learning materials with success. Using an AI chatbot, it automatically creates succinct notes, summaries, key questions, multiple-choice tests, and contextual responses. The platform dramatically cuts down on study time while enhancing revision quality and knowledge retention when compared to conventional manual learning techniques. Through the use of Retrieval-Augmented Generation, which reduces AI visions and enhances reliability, user testing showed that the system produces accurate responses.

## Limitation
AI processing requires an internet connection.
The quality of the files uploaded determines performance.
Large videos could take longer to process.
Low-quality scanned documents reduce OCR accuracy.
Only a few languages are supported in the current edition.
The accuracy of uploaded learning resources determines AI answers.
